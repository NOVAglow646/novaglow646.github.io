About Me

I'm a second-year Ph.D. student in the School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Peking University. My research interests include:

  • Mechanism and applications of LLMs and multimodal LLMs: Including in-context learning (ICL), hallucination, complex reasoning and test-time scaling techniques.
  • Theory and algorithmic design of OOD generalization: Including generalization problems across various machine learning models and scenarios, like domain generalization (DG) in computer vision tasks, graph OOD generalization, and generalization of LLMs.
